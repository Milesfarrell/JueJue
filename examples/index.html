Background

EAP

At Aspire Therapy, I’ve created a welcoming, non-clinical space where young people feel comfortable and safe. If your child isn’t comfortable talking or needs help to get started, I offer Equine Assisted Therapy (EAP), using the eagala model, a powerful experiential therapy, with a hands-on approach.
